More about me

I am an Ottawa Valley raised, sport playing, hell raisin’ kinda gal. Nothing beats a great bonfire and a couple beers with some good friends (except for netflix, sometimes netflix wins).

I played competitive sports since I can remember. (It may be because any game in my house was a competitive one, but that’s for a later time). Basketball was my game but I dabbled in football, badminton, volleyball, track, and swimming and anything else I may be able to win. Sports taught me that a little teamwork and a some good competition make for a deadly combination.

I am an opinionated, optimistic, brutaly honest, proud Canadian designer with a passion to push the limits. I am me.
